Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before your first session.

How do I book?
+
Browse services, click Book Now, fill in the form, and pay your deposit via Bitcoin or USDT. We confirm within 24 hours.
How much is the deposit?
+
The deposit is 50% of the session price. For example a $100 session requires a $50 deposit. The remaining balance is paid on the day.
What should I do before my appointment?
+
Arrive a few minutes early, avoid heavy meals 1 hour before, stay hydrated, and share any health conditions at the start of your session.
Is the deposit refundable?
+
Deposits are non-refundable but fully transferable. Reschedule at least 48 hours in advance and your deposit rolls over, no questions asked.
Do you offer couples massage?
+
Yes! Mention it in your booking notes and we will arrange everything for your session together.
Why crypto payments only?
+
Crypto is fast, secure, and private with no bank delays or processing fees, keeping deposits simple for clients anywhere in the world.
